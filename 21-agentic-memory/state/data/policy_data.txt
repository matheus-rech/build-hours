
# **Laptop Refund & Return Policy**

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025
**Applies To:** All laptop computers and laptop accessories purchased directly from **[Company Name]**, including online, in-store, enterprise, educational, and promotional sales.

This Refund & Return Policy (“Policy”) governs the eligibility, process, and conditions under which customers may request a refund, replacement, store credit, or repair for laptop products purchased from [Company Name]. By completing a purchase, the customer acknowledges and agrees to all terms outlined herein.

---

## **1. Definitions**

For the purpose of this Policy:

* **“Laptop”** refers to any portable personal computing device sold by [Company Name], including notebooks, ultrabooks, gaming laptops, workstations, and 2-in-1 convertible devices.
* **“Customer”** refers to the original purchaser whose name appears on the invoice.
* **“Original Condition”** means the laptop is returned with no physical damage, no software tampering, no missing components, and in the same cosmetic and functional state as delivered.
* **“Refund”** refers to the reimbursement of funds to the original payment method.
* **“Return”** refers to the physical shipment or in-store surrender of the laptop back to [Company Name].
* **“RMA”** means Return Merchandise Authorization, a mandatory tracking number issued prior to any return.

---

## **2. Standard Return Window**

### 2.1 Consumer Purchases

Customers may request a return for a refund within **30 calendar days** from the original delivery date.

### 2.2 Business & Enterprise Purchases

Enterprise, bulk, contract, and B2B purchases are eligible for return within **14 calendar days**, unless otherwise stated in a custom enterprise agreement.

### 2.3 Educational & Promotional Sales

All educational discounts, clearance items, special promotions, flash sales, and refurbished units are limited to a **7-day return window** unless explicitly stated otherwise at the time of sale.

---

## **3. Eligibility Requirements for Refund**

To qualify for a refund, **all** of the following conditions must be met:

1. The laptop must be returned **within the applicable return window**.
2. The laptop must be in **original physical condition**, free of:

   * Cracks, dents, scratches, liquid damage, burn marks, or impact damage
   * Evidence of overclocking, BIOS tampering, or hardware modification
3. All original components must be included, including:

   * Power adapter
   * Battery (if removable)
   * Manuals
   * Warranty cards
   * Stickers
   * Protective films (if applicable)
4. Original packaging must be included and in resalable condition.
5. Any **preinstalled security seals** must be intact.
6. The device must not be:

   * Reported stolen
   * Subject to fraud investigation
   * Locked by third-party security software without customer removing it

Failure to meet **any** of the above criteria may result in:

* A partial refund
* A restocking fee
* Store credit only
* Or outright denial of refund

---

## **4. Non-Returnable and Non-Refundable Items**

The following items are strictly **non-refundable**:

* Customized or build-to-order laptops (CPU, RAM, GPU, or storage customized)
* Software licenses, digital subscriptions, and operating system upgrades
* Items damaged by:

  * Liquid exposure
  * Electrical surges
  * Fire
  * Unauthorized repair
* Items returned without an RMA
* Items returned with missing serial numbers or altered serial labels
* Clearance items marked **Final Sale**
* Laptops used for:

  * Mining
  * Penetration testing
  * High-risk industrial environments
  * Military or classified applications

* Return shipping is the **customer’s responsibility** unless:

  * The product is defective
  * The return resulted from a shipping error
* If a prepaid return label is issued:

  * Damaged shipments void refund eligibility

---

## **8. Defective Products (DOA & Functional Failures)**

### 8.1 Dead on Arrival (DOA)

A laptop is considered DOA if it fails to power on within **48 hours of first delivery**.

DOA claims require:

* Video proof of failure
* Original delivery timestamp
* Serial number verification

Approved DOA claims are eligible for:

* Full refund
* Free replacement
* Expedited shipping (at company discretion)

### 8.2 Post-DOA Defects

Hardware failures after 48 hours fall under **manufacturer warranty**, not refund eligibility.

---

## **9. Warranty vs Refund Distinction**

Refund eligibility ends after the return window. Thereafter:

* Hardware issues are processed through **warranty service**
* Software issues are governed by the **software vendor**
* Data loss, corruption, or cyber incidents are **customer responsibility**

Warranty service does **not** entitle the customer to a refund.

## **15. Return Authorization (RMA) Process**

All returns must follow this process:

1. Submit return request through official support channels
2. Provide:

   * Order number
   * Serial number
   * Reason for return
3. Receive RMA number
4. Ship product within **7 days** of RMA issuance
5. Track shipment until confirmed received

Returns without valid RMA are automatically rejected.
